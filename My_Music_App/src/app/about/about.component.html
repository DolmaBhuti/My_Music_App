About Us

Welcome to my Angular-based music discovery platform, where we connect you to the world of music through the power of the Spotify API. Our app is designed to offer you an immersive experience, allowing you to explore new releases, discover albums, and connect with your favorite artists seamlessly.
At the heart of our application lies a custom authorization Node API. We've crafted this API to ensure the highest standards of security and functionality. Leveraging Redis server for JWT refresh tokens, we've implemented a robust authentication system, prioritizing user privacy and data protection.
Furthermore, our application harnesses the flexibility and scalability of MongoDB to manage user data and preferences efficiently. Your favorite tracks are securely stored within our MongoDB database, ensuring a personalized experience every time you use the app.
With a sleek Angular interface, our platform offers intuitive navigation, allowing you to effortlessly browse through new releases, explore albums, and delve into the diverse world of artists.
